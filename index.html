DASHTWO — Your AI Wingman

Powered by AWS Bedrock. Trained on aviation. Integrated into your logbook.

Not a Chatbot

DASHTWO is an AI copilot integrated directly into the AeroEdge ecosystem. It doesn't give generic answers — it's a context-aware assistant that understands your logbook, your aircraft, your training progress, and your goals.

Every other aviation AI is a generic chatbot. DASHTWO knows YOUR logbook, YOUR aircraft, YOUR currencies. It doesn't guess — it calculates from your actual data.

What DASHTWO Does

  • Analyzes your flight history for proficiency trends
  • Suggests training focus areas based on your actual data
  • Answers aviation regulation questions with CFR citations
  • Helps plan currency restoration flights
  • Reviews logbook entries for inconsistencies
  • Provides weather briefing summaries tied to your planned flights
