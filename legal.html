Privacy Policy

Who We Are

TALLYAERO ("AeroEdge," "we," "us," or "our") operates the TallyAero website (tallyaero.com) and the AeroEdge Logbook web application (aeroedge-logbook.web.app). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. Phone number is optional and only collected if you choose to enable SMS notifications.

Logbook Data: Flight entries, pilot profile, certificates, aircraft information, and documents you upload.

Usage Data: Basic analytics (via Google Analytics) to understand how the site and app are used.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the logbook application
  • To send notification emails if you opt in (e.g., currency expiration reminders, medical certificate alerts, document expiration warnings)
  • To send SMS text messages if you opt in (e.g., FAA currency alerts, medical certificate reminders, aircraft maintenance notifications)
  • To respond to your support inquiries
  • To improve our products

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud). Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256 encryption. Sensitive documents are encrypted at rest. Your authentication tokens, draft entries, and local cache remain on your device only.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services solely for app functionality:

  • Firebase (Google Cloud) — Authentication and data storage
  • SendGrid — Email notifications (only if you enable email notifications)
  • Twilio — SMS text message notifications (only if you enable SMS notifications)
  • Google Analytics — Website usage analytics

We never sell your data. These services are used solely for app functionality.

Data Ownership & Export

Your data is yours. The app supports local-first storage and full data export. You can use the application entirely offline without syncing to the cloud. If you choose to delete your account, all cloud-stored data will be removed.

SMS/Text Message Notifications

SMS notifications are entirely optional and disabled by default. To receive SMS messages, you must:

  1. Navigate to Notification Settings within the app
  2. Enter your phone number
  3. Check the consent checkbox explicitly agreeing to receive text messages
  4. Verify your phone number with a 6-digit code sent via SMS

Types of SMS messages: FAA currency expiration alerts, medical certificate reminders, document expiration warnings, and aircraft maintenance notifications.

Message frequency: Varies based on your notification preferences, typically 1–10 messages per month.

Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time, or disable SMS notifications in your app settings. Reply HELP for help. You can also contact us at info@flyaeroedge.com for assistance.

We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your phone number is used exclusively for delivering the notifications you have opted into.

Cookies

We use cookies for authentication (to keep you signed in) and analytics (Google Analytics). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at info@flyaeroedge.com.
